ADC

Champion Pros Cons
Jinx
  • Insane late game damage
  • Excellent wave clear with rockets
  • High damage poke with her W ability
  • Great at destroying towers
  • Low mobility
  • No form of escape, unless you get a kill/tower
  • Low range in minigun mode
  • Can burn through mana if you don't play conservative
Sivir
  • High burst damage
  • Has the ability to push lanes effectively
  • Has spell shield that blocks enemy abilities
  • Ulimate gives team great mobility
  • Boomerang is a skill shot
  • Has mana problems
  • Must time spell shield within 3 seconds
  • Medium attack range for an ADC
Vayne
  • Deals a certain percentage of true damage based on enemys health
  • Highest single target damage
  • Really strong late game
  • Great kiting ability
  • Great at objective taking (baron/dragon)
  • Difficult laning phase due to small attack range
  • Requires good positiong to make plays
  • Lowest base stats of any ADC
  • Dependent on support
Caitlyn
  • One of the longest auto attack ranges
  • Traps can help protect you in lane
  • Good escape with use of E ability
  • Can push lane quickly
  • Provies great poke in lane
  • No steroid
  • Not as much sustain damage as other ADC
