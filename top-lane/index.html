Top Lane

Champion Pros Cons
Garen
  • Naturally tanky due to his W ability which grants armor and magic resistance on minion kills
  • Lots of regen for the laning phase with his passive which boots his regen when out o combat
  • Heavy burst with his full combo
  • Lots of mobility with his Q movement speed increase and slow removal
  • A lot of early game damge/burst
  • Damage falls of late game
  • Laning phase can be countered by alot of champions
Wukong
  • High burst damage
  • Stealth
  • Higher than average melee range
  • Short cooldowns on his high damage skills
  • Increadible AoE damage (4.4 ratio)
  • Inate armor/mr (passive)
  • Hard to catch when his clone is up
  • Level 1 ulitemate is underwhelming
  • No "flash like" get away
  • Gets countered by good vision control on the enemy team espcially use of pink wards in team fights
Malphite
  • Free AD and Armor
  • Super tanky
  • Great passive that gives shield
  • Has great escape with ult
  • Felxible builds
  • Great engage with ultimate if teammates follow up
  • Hard to lane against ranged champions
  • Shield takes 10 seconds to come back
  • CC can ruin the timing on your ult Unstoppable force
  • Hard to carry games when compared to other top laners
  • Can lose if you don't time your ultimate correctly
Riven
  • Very easy to snowball in lane
  • High mobility
  • Deals high amounts of damage if you don't fall behind
  • Every skill scales with AD
  • Cool Down Reduction is broken on this champion
  • Needs alot of farm or kills to stay relevant
  • Late game teammate fights enemy team will focus you
  • Needs flash to get on carries in team fights
  • Little room for error when it comes to positioning yourself in team fights
